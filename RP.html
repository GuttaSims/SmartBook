Atlanta Police Department Codes

Study these, as you will need to know how and what to report to dispatch!

1: NON-EMERGENCY
2: EMERGENCY - lights and sirens are NOT REQUIRED
3: EMERGENCY - lights and sirens ARE REQUIRED
4: CANCEL CALL
5: DOMESTIC
6: FALSE ALARM
6S: FALSE ALARM (WEATHER STORM)
7: AVAILABLE FOR SERVICE
8: PERSON "GONE"
9: TRANSFER RESPONSIBILITY
10: VEHICLE IMPOUND
11: INFORMATION
12: NO ARREST / NO REPORT
13: NOT VALID ADDRESS
14: ALARM REFUSED
14a: ALARM REFUSED - ALARM OUT
14b: ALARM REFUSED - ALARM RESET
15: ALARM - OWNER ON WAY
15a: ALARM OWNER ON WAY - ALARM OUT
15b: ALARM OWNER ON WAY - ALARM RESET
16: RELEASE STOLEN PROPERTY TO OWNER
17: REPORT MADE
17F: USED FORCE TO AFFECT ARREST
17D: REPORT - PRE-ARREST DIVERSION PROGRAM
17P: VEHICLE PURSUIT
19: STOLEN ARTICLE
20: O.C. USED
20T: ECD DEPLOYMENT
21: KIDNAPPING - OFFICER
22: CAN'T LOCATE ADDRESS
23: ARREST AND COPY OF CHARGES
24: NOTHING TO CALL
25: LOCATED EXPLOSIVE DEVICE OR SUSPECTED EXPLOSIVE DEVICE
26: ARRIVE ON CALL
28: WANTED CHECK
30: MAYOR OR MAYOR'S FAMILY INJURED
31: REMOVE SHOTGUN / PATROL RIFLE
