USED 20T: ECD DEPLOYMENT 21: KIDNAPPING - OFFICER 22: CAN'T LOCATE ADDRESS 23: ARREST AND COPY OF CHARGES 24: NOTHING TO CALL 25: LOCATED EXPLOSIVE DEVICE OR SUSPECTED EXPLOSIVE DEVICE 26: ARRIVE ON CALL 28: WANTED CHECK 30: MAYOR OR MAYOR'S FAMILY INJURED 31: REMOVE SHOTGUN / PATROL RIFLE
